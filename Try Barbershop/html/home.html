About Our Barber Shop

At Twin's Barbershop and Jake's Tattoo, we believe a great haircut is about more than just looks—it's about feeling confident and at home. Our team of skilled barbers blends modern style with traditional techniques to give you the perfect cut every time. More than just a barbershop, we’re a community space where conversations flow, stories are shared, and everyone is treated like family. Whether you’re here for a fresh fade, a beard trim, or a classic cut, we’re here to make sure you leave feeling your best. Welcome to Twin's Barbershop and Jake's Tattoo—where style meets community.
